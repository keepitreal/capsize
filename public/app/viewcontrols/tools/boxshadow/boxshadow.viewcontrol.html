Box Shadow Generator

Box shadow is a feature from the CSS3 specification that describes one or more box shadow (also known as 'drop shadow' in other contexts) effects that can be applied as a property to an HTML element. Multiple box shadow effects are achieved through a comma-separated list of values on the 'box-shadow'. Earlier box-shadow effects are stacked/z-indexed above
